ABOUT ME

It has always been about finding my purpose in this world. This has led me to where I am now, pursuing a Master of Science in Robotics at Northeastern University, Boston. I also hold a Bachelor of Technology in Electronics and Communication Engineering from National Institute of Technology, Surat, famously known as SVNIT.

My passion about Robotics had me exploring it early in my undergraduate which helped me gain experience through several research projects. Inclined to further venture into the world of robotics, I took it upon myself to develop a "Pothole Detector using ROS and OpenCV/Deep Learning" for my final year thesis wherein I integrated SLAM, object avoidance and navigation algorithms.

I am the Co-Founder of Plutus Security Solutions, a company focusing on providing novel Security Solutions such as Perimeter Intrusion Detection System. Therein, I learnt not only about developing a product from prototype but also about presenting and pitching the product to various organisations and companies to secure funding. It also helped me cultivate leadership skills as a byproduct of being the Co-Founder. My greatest achievement as a part of this company was to secure a funding of approximately 121,000 USD (INR 90 lakhs).

An Engineer by Education and an Entrepreneur at Heart, I am always looking for opportunities that can help me grow as a person and a Roboticist while also making social contribution for the betterment of humanity.
